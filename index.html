A word from our designer

Back Bone is more than a name, it is a way of being...

A brand that emulates everything it means to be a strong woman. This brand is dedicated to all women, who show their strength every day. When you wear a Back Bone piece you are a part of all those females of the past, present and future. It was inspired by my grandmothers Theresa and Ann Faye.

Both women were instrumental in making me who I am today. They always taught me to show some back bone meaning to stand strong, believe in yourself, and never let others break you. I have carried their message throughout my life and wanted a brand that showed tribute to the women who have shaped me.

So, show some back bone.
